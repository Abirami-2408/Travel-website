City Palace , Museum Udaipur.

The City Palace Museum , Udaipur is housed within very iconic , 450 years old Palace, the former abode of the rulers of Mewar. Adults pay $300 , children(5-12 yrs) and students with a valid ID pay $150.Children under five the ticket be free.

India Gate,New Delhi

The Best time to visit:October to March. Open 24 hours , but the light shows and boating have specific timing. Wear comfortable shoes for walking.Carry a waterbottle.Free Entry The boating charge of India gate is Rs.50 per person for a ride of 15 minutes while for 30 minutes boat ride,Rs.100 per person.

Lotus Temple,New Delhi,India.

The lotus like shape, it has prominant attraction in the city. The Lotus Temple is open to all, regardless of religion or any other qualification. The building is composed of 27 free-standing marble-clad "petals" arranged in clusters of three to form nine sides,with nine doors opening onto a center hall.

Price: $500

Buddha Park,Buddhist temple in Sikkim.

The Buddha Park is located approximately 1 kilometre north of the city of Ravangla, in the South Sikkim district of India's Sikkim state The Buddhist circuit of the park was built under a state government project,intended to boost pilgrimage and tourism to the region.
